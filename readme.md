	<h2 align="center"> Summarizer </h2>
	<h4 align="center"> Summarizer gives you a summary or gist of a very long text. You can use it  to quickly know what a long article, essay, research paper or news article wants to convey. It saves time for the user. This repo contain the backend of the summarizer app.<h4>
